## 💼 Sample Prompts

### Prompt #1: Marketing Strategy Builder
```
As a small business marketing consultant, create a 90-day 
marketing strategy for a [business type] that serves 
[target audience]. 

Include:
1. 3 main marketing channels to focus on
2. Weekly content themes
3. Budget allocation recommendations
4. Key metrics to track
5. Timeline with specific milestones
```

### Prompt #2: Cold Email Templates
```
Create a sequence of 5 cold outreach emails for a [your industry] 
business targeting [ideal client profile]. 

Make it:
- Professional but conversational
- Under 150 words per email
- Include personalization tips
- Have a clear call to action in each email
```
