## 🎯 Sample Prompt: Viral Video Topic Generator

```
I am a [your niche] content creator on [YouTube/TikTok/Instagram]. 
My target audience is [describe your ideal viewer: age, interests, pain points].

Generate 15 viral-ready video topics that:
- Solve a specific problem my audience has
- Have high search potential
- Are unique and different from competitors
- Can be done with [your resources/setup]

For each topic include:
1. Video Title
2. Hook (first 3 seconds)
3. Main points to cover
4. Call to action
5. Estimated video length
```
