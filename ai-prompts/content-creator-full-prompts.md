## CATEGORY 1: VIDEO IDEAS & HOOKS

### Prompt 1: Viral Video Topic Generator
```
Create 15 viral-ready video topics for a [niche] YouTuber/TikToker.

Target audience: [describe age, interests, pain points]

For each topic provide:
1. Video title (attention-grabbing)
2. Hook (first 3 seconds)
3. Main points to cover (3-5)
4. Call to action
5. Estimated length

Make sure topics:
- Solve a specific problem
- Have search potential
- Are unique from competitors
- Can be filmed with basic equipment
```

### Prompt 2: Hook & Title Builder
```
Create 10 attention-grabbing hooks and titles for a [type] video about [topic].

Include:
- 5 hook formulas (under 10 seconds)
- 5 title formulas (under 60 characters)
- Why each works psychologically
- Platform-specific adaptations for YouTube, TikTok, Instagram

Target audience: [describe]
Goal: [views/engagement/conversions]
```

### Prompt 3: Trending Topic Finder
