## Prompt 1: Business Name Generator
```

[target audience] with [main benefit]. 

Generate 20 creative, memorable business names that:
- Are easy to pronounce and spell
- Convey trust and professionalism
- Have available .com domain
- Work well for social media handles

For each name, provide:
1. The name
2. A brief explanation of the meaning
3. Available domain (.com availability)
4. 3 potential taglines
```

## Prompt 2: SWOT Analysis Builder
```
Create a comprehensive SWOT analysis for my [type of business].
I specialize in [your services/target market].

Help me identify:
- STRENGTHS: My unique skills, advantages, resources
- WEAKNESSES: Areas I need to improve, gaps in my offerings
- OPPORTUNITIES: Market trends, unmet needs, growth areas
- THREATS: Competitors, market challenges, external factors

For each quadrant, provide 5 specific points with detailed explanations.
```
